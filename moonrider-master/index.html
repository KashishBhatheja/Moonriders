About Us

Moonriders is a SAE Collegiate club of JECRC Foundation, founded in Year 2009. It is re-established in year 2012 and has been on path of improvement and learning since then. We started with few students and now we have grown to a club of more than 80 students and 7 teams working on multiple projects. We hope to continue this trend.
